Crownless Knight

A Kingdom Lost,
A Knight Reborn

Years ago, a peaceful village was destroyed by an army of the undead. Only one survived — a young boy who witnessed the massacre. With his home reduced to ashes and his family gone, the boy carried that pain into adulthood.

Now known as the Crownless Knight, he has trained in battle and hardened his resolve. Guided by loss and fueled by anger, he sets out on a dangerous journey to confront the Undead King — the one responsible for the destruction of his world — and bring an end to his reign.

"He wears no crown, swears no oath to any lord — only to the dead who cannot speak for themselves."

Navigate treacherous biomes, survive relentless enemy waves, build your arsenal, and face three devastating bosses standing between you and your destiny.

Game Identity

Four Pillars of Gameplay

Crownless Knight fuses four distinct genres into a seamless, high-octane experience.

⚡
Arcade Action

Fast-paced, top-down combat in 3D arenas where movement, positioning, and sharp reflexes are essential for survival.

🎲
Roguelite

Persistent progression through runs where players select different attribute builds to define their playstyle before entering the dungeon.

🏛️
Dungeon Crawler

Exploration through three distinct, increasingly difficult biomes — Plains, Forest, and Dungeon — culminating in intense boss encounters.

🤝
Co-op Multiplayer

Peer-to-peer architecture allowing 1-2 players to navigate dangerous stages, share enemy encounters, and fight together in real-time.

The World

Three Deadly Biomes

A 15-stage campaign across three increasingly dangerous regions, each culminating in a devastating boss encounter.

Stages 1 – 5

The Plains

The introduction to the game's core mechanics. Open terrain and scattered ruins allow players to move freely while learning enemy behaviors. Ideal for mastering auto-combat positioning and build progression.

The Forest

Tighter arenas, denser enemy waves, and more aggressive combinations. Visibility is reduced by thick trees and environmental obstacles, increasing pressure and limiting safe movement paths.

Co-op

Multiplayer Systems

Designed to test team synergy, spatial awareness, and resource management. Fight side-by-side in local or online co-op.

🖥️
Local Co-op (Split-Screen)

Dynamic split-screen that merges when players are close and splits when they separate. Supports Keyboard + Gamepad or dual Gamepads.

🌐
Online Co-op (P2P)

Peer-to-peer architecture ensures responsive, real-time combat synchronization. Both players progress together through the same stages and bosses.

💰
Shared Economy

Gold dropped by enemies is shared — added to a collective bank or duplicated for both players, ensuring fair upgrade access during Shop Phases.

⚖️
Build Synergy

Players choose attributes independently. A Tank can hold the frontline while a Ranger or Assassin deals DPS from the backline.

📈
Dynamic Scaling

Enemy health, spawn rates, and elite frequency scale by 50–75% when two players are active. Bosses actively switch aggro between players.

💗
Revive Mechanic

When a player is downed, their partner can revive them by standing in a revival radius for 3–5 seconds. If both are downed simultaneously, the run ends.

Game Modes

Campaign & Endless

Two distinct modes — story-driven progression or infinite survival. Choose your challenge.

📜
Campaign Mode

The structured story progression of Crownless Knight. Survive 15 stages across three biomes and defeat the Undead King to claim your revenge.

  • 15 total stages across Plains, Forest, and Dungeon
  • 3 unique boss encounters (Stage 5, 10, and 15)
  • Shop Phase between every stage
  • Victory sequence with run statistics on completion
  • Option to continue into Endless Mode after finishing
∞
Endless Mode

Remove the stage limit and survive for as long as possible. Difficulty scales infinitely — how far can you push your build?

  • Unlimited waves with ever-increasing difficulty
  • Higher enemy density, stronger tiers, more elites
  • Shops still appear between waves for continued scaling
  • Leaderboard-style high score progression
  • Pure build optimization and survival challenge
